# Bike Sales Analysis using Python

## 📌 Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a bike sales dataset to uncover meaningful business insights. Using Python and data visualization techniques, the analysis identifies sales trends, brand performance, customer preferences, and market distribution.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that can support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Explore and understand the bike sales dataset.
* Analyze sales performance across different brands.
* Identify top-performing states and market share.
* Study owner types, seller types, and fuel preferences.
* Visualize key business insights through charts.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

* Data Cleaning & Exploration
* Brand-wise Total Sales Revenue
* Brand Market Share Analysis
* State-wise Sales Performance
* Manufacturing Year Analysis
* Owner Type Distribution
* Average Selling Price by Brand
* Fuel Type Distribution
* Seller Type Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified the brand generating the highest total sales revenue.
* Compared average selling prices across different brands.
* Analyzed market share distribution among bike manufacturers.
* Observed customer preferences based on owner and seller types.
* Examined geographical sales trends across states.
